No. The free Notion account works perfectly. You can duplicate and use the entire OS without paying Notion a dime.

Both. The system was built in an embedded model that bridges agency and in-house recruiting. The pipeline tracker, scoring frameworks, and prompt library work regardless of your setup.

Email us. This was built from a real practice, so if something doesn't fit your workflow, we want to hear about it. We're here to help you make it work.

The free tier of Claude works for most prompts. A paid subscription gives you longer outputs and faster responses, but it's not required to get value from the prompt library.

Absolutely. Once you duplicate the template into your workspace, it's yours. Add fields, change stages, modify templates — make it fit your exact workflow.
